What is Restore Purchases?

Restore Purchases allows you to recover your previously purchased ONYX subscription on a new device or after reinstalling the app. This feature is provided in accordance with Apple App Store guidelines.

When to Use Restore Purchases

Use this feature if:

  • You reinstalled the ONYX app
  • You got a new iPhone or iPad
  • You reset your device to factory settings
  • Your subscription is not showing as active
  • You were previously a subscriber and want to check your status

How to Restore Purchases

1Open ONYX

Launch the ONYX app on your device.

2Go to Settings

Tap the Settings tab in the bottom navigation bar.

3Find Restore Option

Scroll down to find the "Restore Purchases" option.

4Tap Restore

Tap the "Restore Purchases" button.

5Authenticate

You may be prompted to sign in with your Apple ID. Use the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase.

6Confirmation

Once complete, you will see a confirmation message and your subscription will be restored.

Important Information

Apple ID Requirement

  • You must use the same Apple ID that was used for the original purchase
  • Purchases cannot be transferred between Apple IDs
  • Family Sharing subscriptions must be restored on each family member's device

Subscription Status

After restoring:

  • Your subscription status will be updated immediately
  • All premium features will be unlocked
  • Your billing cycle remains unchanged

What Gets Restored

Troubleshooting

"No Purchases to Restore"

This message appears when:

  • You never had an active subscription on this Apple ID
  • Your subscription has expired
  • You're signed in with a different Apple ID

Solution:

  1. Check which Apple ID you used for the original purchase
  2. Sign out and sign in with the correct Apple ID
  3. Try restoring again

"Unable to Connect"

This error indicates a network issue.

Solution:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try connecting to a different network
  3. Wait a few minutes and try again

"Subscription Not Recognized"

Your subscription may have expired or been cancelled.

Solution:

  1. Check your subscription status in iOS Settings
  2. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  3. Look for ONYX in your subscription list
  4. Resubscribe if your subscription has ended

Still Having Issues?

  3. Restart your device
  4. Check for app updates in the App Store

Managing Your Subscription

View Subscription Status

In the App:

  1. Open ONYX
  2. Go to Settings
  3. View your current subscription status

In iOS Settings:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find ONYX in the list

Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID (your name at the top)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select ONYX
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Note: You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Change Subscription Plan

To change your subscription plan:

  1. Open the ONYX app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Manage Subscription
  4. Select a different plan

Or through iOS Settings: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → ONYX

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to pay again after restoring?

A: No. Restoring purchases recovers your existing active subscription at no additional cost.

Q: Can I use my subscription on multiple devices?

A: Yes. You can use your subscription on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

Q: What happens if my subscription expired?

A: Restore Purchases will not reactivate an expired subscription. You will need to resubscribe.

Q: Can I transfer my subscription to a different Apple ID?

A: No. Apple subscriptions are tied to the Apple ID used for purchase and cannot be transferred.

Q: Will I lose my favorites and collections?

A: No. If you're signed in with the same account (Apple, Google, or email), your favorites and collections are synced to the cloud and will be restored.

Q: How long does restore take?

A: Typically instant. In rare cases, it may take a few minutes for Apple's servers to verify your purchase.
